# Creative Coding Experiment

This project is my personal exploration into **interactive generative systems** using **p5.js**. It started as a simple experiment inspired by a **blob tutorial on YouTube** after a workshop by Chris on hand-tracking and creative coding. From there, I challenged myself to expand the concept through **11 creative iterations**, learning step by step and experimenting with interactivity, mouse control and ultimately **hand tracking**.

---

## Project Overview

- **Purpose:** Experiment with interactive digital environments and learn about hand tracking and generative systems.
- **Tools:** p5.js, JavaScript (`sketch.js`)
- **Focus:** Conceptual growth through iterative experimentation and exploration of human-computer interaction.

---

## Iterations and Conceptual Growth

1. **Iteration 01 – A Blob**  
   - The first step: creating a simple blob based on a tutorial.

2. **Iteration 02 – Interactive Blob**  
   - The blob now responds to mouse interaction, creating the first sense of interactivity.

3. **Iteration 03 – Blob Ecosystem**  
   - Multiple blobs interact with each other, forming a small ecosystem.

4. **Iteration 04 – Personal Space**  
   - Explores the idea of personal space in digital environments.  
   - Instead of directly controlling the blobs, the user influences the environment simply by moving through it.  
   - Blobs instinctively move away from the cursor, creating space wherever the user goes.  

**Conceptual metaphor:**  
- Walking through a field of blobs is like walking through a crowd of people who instinctively make space for you.  
- This iteration reflects **personal space**, **human presence**, and **how systems respond to humans**.  

**Details of Interaction:**  
- Many small and large blobs spread across the canvas  
- Blobs move lightly and organically  
- When the cursor gets close → blobs move away  
- When the cursor moves away → blobs slowly return  
- Creates a feeling of a **living, responsive environment**

5. **Iterations 08–11**  
   - Further experimentation and refinement, gradually incorporating more **complex interactions** and ultimately **hand tracking**.

---

## Key Learnings

- **Iterative Design:** Each step built upon the last, showing clear conceptual growth from a single blob to a responsive ecosystem.  
- **Interactivity:** Learned to make digital elements respond in ways that feel intuitive and organic.  
- **Hand Tracking:** Explored how physical movements can directly influence digital environments.  
- **Creative Coding:** Gained hands-on experience in p5.js and generative systems.
